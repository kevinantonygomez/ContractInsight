Appendix: Explanation of Legal Terms

- Fixed-Term Agreement (Lease): A contract for a specific period, after which it may renew or terminate.

- Month-to-Month Agreement: A rental agreement that continues each month until terminated by either party with notice.

- Security Deposit: Money held by the landlord to cover potential property damage or unpaid rent.

- Grace Period: An additional time period allowed to make a payment without penalty.

- Abandonment: Tenant's action indicating they have given up rights to the property.

- Disclosure: Providing necessary information to ensure both parties are fully informed.
